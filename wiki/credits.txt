<p>OpenRDK is the result of the development activity in the <a href="http://sied.dis.uniroma1.it/">SIED Laboratory</a>, whose coordinator is <a href="http://www.dis.uniroma1.it/~nardi">Prof. Daniele Nardi.</a>.</p>
